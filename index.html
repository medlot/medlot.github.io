FOR COMPANIES

Great opportunity to improve your user-centered service development and customer experience! We’re looking for companies to provide interesting and real customer experience related projects for students.

How does your business benefit
from Summer of CX?

Team of students can conduct online or qualitative research on your digital service, prototype or concept, for example. Other assignments can include mystery shopping that offers valuable information of your current customer experience in stores!
You will get profitable feedback, new perspective and service development ideas during the summer.
You will have a great chance to get in touch with the future professionals.

A good customer experience fulfills user’s autonomy, competence and relatedness. Customer experience encompasses all interaction throughout the physical and digital touch-points of the customer journey. Usability, functionality, amenity and reliability will be a centre of attention when providing a good customer experience before, during and after the usage of the product or service.

FOR Students

Spend a few summer days on a genuine user-centred evaluation project!

What do you get from Summer of CX?

Not only will you gain great experience and meet new friends but also receive a diploma to attach to your CV.

Most of all, you will have an opportunity to familiarize yourself with the company representatives as a potential future employers.

You are supported with mentoring and continuous feedback by User Intelligence professionals. You will be also able to utilize the full potential of Microsoft Flux, a new community space to network and explore their business ideas.

The projects consist of evaluations and light user studies done by the student teams. The evaluations will be done in small groups according to timeframes suitable to you. The average workload will be approximately 3-5 days of work during summer per student depending on your team’s ambition. The topics are provided by the participating companies and are announced in the kick-off event in June. The projects will be carried out in Finnish or in English, depending on the preferences of the teams and the companies.

Besides working with your team, there will be two events organized for the participants, the Kick-off event in June and the CX Gala in August. These events will be organized in the premises of Microsoft in Helsinki city center.
